The design of this site is heavily inspired by [Andy Matuschaks'](https://andymatuschak.org/). I really like its use of horizontal space. It's built in [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) using [Jekyll Now](https://github.com/barryclark/jekyll-now) (though has gone through much revision) and is hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/andrewrlynch/andrewrlynch.github.io). The primary font is in Alegreya Sans and the color scheme is inspired by the [Financial Times](https://www.ft.com/).

I see this site as the central hub for the many things I'm interested in, personally and professionally, and intend to develop it as somewhat of a [digital garden](https://www.technologyreview.com/2020/09/03/1007716/digital-gardens-let-you-cultivate-your-own-little-bit-of-the-internet/).
