#### Ingredients
1 bar spoon amarenna cherry syrup <br>
2 dashes Fee Brothers Orange Bitters <br>
4 dashes Angostura Bitters <br>
0.25 oz Cocchi Vermouth di Torino <br>
2 oz Four Roses Kentucky Bourbon or Knob Creek Small Batch Rye <br>
1/2 shaker/glass ice <br>
Twist of orange <br>
Amarenna cherry

#### Directions
In a shaker or bar glass 1/2 full of ice, combine cherry syrup, orange and Angostura bitters, vermouth, and bourbon or rye. Stir 30-40 times to chill and dilute. Serve up in couple glass with twist of orange and an amarenna cherry. Level this up with Luxardo maraschino cherries if you wish. The improvement is not worth the price IMHO.
