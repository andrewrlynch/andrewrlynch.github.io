Herbal, bittersweet, and refreshing.
#### Ingredients
3/4 shaker of ice <br>
0.25 - 0.5 oz honey syrup (1:1 honey:water) <br>
0.25 - 0.5 oz lemon juice <br>
5 dashes Fee Brothers Orange Bitters <br>
1.5 oz Seedlip Garden 108 <br>
A splash of the spiciest ginger beer/ale you can find.

#### Directions
In a cocktail shaker 3/4 full of ice, add honey syrup, lemon juice, orange bitters, and the Seedlip. Shake and serve up or on the rocks with a splash of ginger.
