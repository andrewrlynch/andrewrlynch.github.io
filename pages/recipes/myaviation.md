I prefer mine a bit lighter on the maraschino and a bit more on the floral violette. <br>
2 oz dry gin <br>
0.5 oz lemon juice <br>
0.5 oz elderflower liqueur <br>
0.5 oz crème de violette <br>
1 bar spoon amarenna cherry syrup <br>
1 bar spoon Luxardo maraschino liqueur <br>
1/2 shaker/glass ice <br>
Twist of lemon <br>
Amarenna cherry <br>

Combine. <br>
Add ice. <br>
Shake. <br>
Strain <br>
Garnish.
