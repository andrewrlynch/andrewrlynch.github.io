[ggsuperviolin](https://github.com/andrewrlynch/ggsuperviolin) produces violin superplots for ggplot2. Inspired by the [SuperPlots](https://rupress.org/jcb/article/219/6/e202001064/151717/SuperPlots-Communicating-reproducibility-and) and [Violin SuperPlots](https://www.molbiolcell.org/doi/10.1091/mbc.E21-03-0130) editorials, I wanted a way to create violin superplots in R. See also the [original Violin SuperPlots package for python](https://github.com/kynnemall/superviolin?tab=readme-ov-file).


<img src="https://github.com/andrewrlynch/ggsuperviolin/raw/main/img/ex4.png" width='576'>

## Installation

``` r
devtools::install_github("andrewrlynch/ggsuperviolin")
```
