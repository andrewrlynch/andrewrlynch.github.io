## Hi!

I received a B.S. in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology at the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire in 2016. Under Dr. Thao Yang, I used solid-phase peptide synthesis and NMR to study the antigenicity of synthesized MUC1 mimotopes. I'm currently a scientist and PhD candidate at the University of Wisconsin - Madison working with Dr. Mark [Burkard](https://www.medicine.wisc.edu/hematology-oncology/welcome-burkard-research-group). I employ genomics, stochastic modeling, and cell biology to study the role of chromosomal instability (CIN) and aneuploidy in the evolution of tumors and the progression of cancer.
